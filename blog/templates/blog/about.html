About Pharm Unique

Pharm. Unique

Licensed Pharmacist | Medical Writer | Health Communicator | Entrepreneur

Hi, I’m Pharm. Unique. A licensed pharmacist, medical writer, health communicator, and entrepreneur passionate about making health education simple, clear, and relatable.

I created this platform to help people like you understand your health, your medications, and your body, without all the confusing jargon. Regardless of the context, be it an article about high blood pressure, sharing tips on drug safety, or creating content for social media, there is just one drive: health education should be realistic and empowering, but not intimidating.

Why I Do What I Do
Having a strong and practical background in pharmaceutical care and public health, I’ve observed how many people are left in the dark about their health. That’s why I’m reconciling the gap between medicine and everyday life, using real-life talk, stories, and realistic tips that make sense and stick in mind.

Currently, I’m writing a series of easy-to-understand medical books that tackle common health conditions from a relatable perspective. The intent is simple: to help people take charge of their health.

My Work So Far
Over the years, I’ve contributed to public health campaigns, including projects with the Ministry of Health, Rivers State, and created engaging and research-driven health content for diverse platforms. I specialize in:

  • Medical writing & content creation
  • Health communication & public education
  • Social media advocacy for health awareness
  • Simplifying drug and disease information

However, I don’t stop at writing. I’m also the founder of ONY Essentials, a Nigerian brand offering affordable, high-quality sanitary and health essentials to help individuals and families stay healthy while living their best life.

More Than a Pharmacist
Beyond the pharmacy coat and pills, I mentor upcoming content creators, helping them build personal brands, create impactful content, and grow their platforms with originality and purpose.

Currently, I’m sharpening my skills in public speaking, personal branding, research tools, and project management. All these, aimed at deepening my impact in public health education and policy.

Let’s Connect
If you’re here to learn, grow, or just to find health tips that change life for the better, you’re in the right place. Welcome to my empire, where learning never ends.
